Arcane Story
Season 1 Act 1
Vi and Powder find their parents dead after a battle in Piltover and are taken in by Vander, a former rebel leader. Years later, the sisters steal from Piltover, but Powder accidentally causes an explosion using magical crystals. Jayce, a Piltover student, is expelled when his arcane experiments are discovered, though Viktor offers to help him continue his work. Meanwhile, crime lord Silco captures Vander after transforming the thug Deckard with a mutagen called shimmer. During a failed rescue attempt, Powder unintentionally kills Mylo and Claggor. Heartbroken, Vi abandons her and is then captured by Marcus, while Silco takes Powder under his wing.

Characters
all the characters in arcane a-z
Amara
  • Notorious for corruption in Piltover
  • Successful trading merchant
  • Described as "greed personified" with obsessive love for Hextech crystals
  • Appears in Arcane episodes 4 and 5
Ambessa
  • Hardened Noxian warlord
  • Mel Medarda's mother
  • Tried to mold Mel into a fighter
  • Tough and familiar with death and destruction
Bolbok
  • Wealthy aristocrat sitting on Piltover's ruling council
  • Driven by fear of Arcane magic
  • Pushed for Jayce's banishment after his Arcane research was exposed
  • Later made business deals with Jayce and negotiated with Silco
  • Present during Jinx's rocket attack on the council building
Caitlyn
  • Also known as 'Cait' or 'Cupcake'
  • Enforcer from the wealthy Kiramman family in Piltover
  • Departs from her parents' elitism to use her privilege for good
  • Driven by truth, justice, and Vi
  • In a relationship with Vi
  • Resourceful and determined to uncover corruption in Piltover
Cassandra
  • Caitlyn's mother
  • Member of Piltover's ruling council
  • Queen of venture capital
  • Became Jayce's patron before turning against him when his Arcane research was revealed
  • Later invited Jayce onto the council after his Hextech stones proved popular
Chross
  • Boss of the Hush Company
  • Born in Piltover but relocated to Silco-ruled undercity
  • Created the first generation of Chem-Barons
  • Appears mainly in The Boy Saviour episode
Claggor
  • Gentle giant and adoptive brother of Vi and Jinx
  • Serves as the muscle of their operations
  • Defended Powder despite her mishandling of crystals
  • Killed by one of Powder's explosions during the raid on Silco's Shimmer factory
Ekko
  • Childhood friend of Vi and Powder
  • Zaunite inventor and leader of the Firelights
  • Hardened by conflict and suffering from a traumatic start to life
  • Dedicated to freeing the undercity from Silco's rule
Elora
  • Mel Medarda's personal assistant
  • Plays crucial behind-the-scenes role in Piltover's political drama
  • Not present in the council room during Jinx's attack
Finn
  • Boss of the Slickjaws and a Chem-Baron
  • Described as fierce, ferocious, and a fighter
  • Known for his sprawling 'Kill' tattoo across his chest and iconic yellow overcoat
  • Killed by Sevika after his failed coup against Silco with Renni
Heimerdinger
  • Highly respected professor, scientist, and former head of Piltover Council
  • Over three centuries old
  • Dedicated to keeping scholars from mixing science and magic
Jayce
  • Known as "The Golden Boy"
  • Famed scientist and innovator
  • Made De-Facto Head of Piltover Council, replacing Heimerdinger
  • Central to much of the action in Arcane
  • Responsible for economic strides in Piltover
Jinx (FKA Powder)
  • Vi's younger sister, formerly known as Powder
  • Orphaned when her parents were killed in a failed uprising against Piltover
  • Became Silco's adopted daughter after separating from Vi
  • Launched a Hextech rocket into the Piltover Council room at the end of Season 1
Marcus
  • Former sheriff of Piltover
  • Had a secret partnership with Silco
  • Lost his position and died when his corruption was discovered
Margot
  • Chem-Baroness and boss of the Vyx
  • Hard-line but ultimately obedient to Silco in Zaun
Mel
  • Noxian aristocrat based in Piltover
  • Member of the ruling council
  • Ambitious and cutthroat in pursuit of her goals
  • Ultimately motivated by protection of Piltover and peace with Zaun
  • Present in the council building during Jinx's attack
Mylo
  • Part of Vi and Powder's gang as an adoptive brother
  • Talented lockpicker
  • Killed by Powder's magical miscalculations with Hextech crystals in Silco's factory
Renni
  • Boss of the Sludgerunners
  • Esteemed Chem-Baroness
  • Works to further Silco's agenda in Zaun against Piltover
  • Failed in coup attempt against Silco
Salo
  • Piltovian aristocrat
  • Member of the ruling council
  • Fate uncertain after Jinx's rocket attack
Sevika
  • Silco's right-hand-woman
  • Top-ranking Zaunite criminal
  • Executes Silco's bidding and dirty work
  • Lost her mechanical arm in a fight with Vi
Silco
  • Main antagonist and ultimate Zaunite Chem-Baron criminal
  • Adoptive father of Jinx
  • Aimed to free Zaun from Piltover's control
  • Killed by Jinx with a bullet through the chest after meeting with Jayce
Singed
  • Also known as "The Doctor"
  • Zaunite scientist who created the drug Shimmer
  • Highly intelligent but lacks empathy
  • Conducts unethical experiments, including on humans
Smeech
  • A Yordle (cyborg) Chem-Baron
  • Boss of the Scrap Hackers
  • Makes a living from crime in Zaun
  • In charge of a group skilled in cybernetic modification
  • Known for having mechanical arms
Tobias
  • Caitlyn's father and Cassandra's husband
  • Piltover native who doesn't feature heavily in the series
  • Invested in Jayce's research alongside his wife
Vander
  • Bartender in Zaun
  • Adoptive father to Vi and Jinx
  • Skilled boxer with a good heart and strong morals
  • Highly respected by undercity residents
  • Presumed dead after an ordeal with Silco and Deckard
Vi
  • Born Violet
  • Hotheaded and fearless fighter
  • Highly skilled in combat, especially with Hextech gauntlets
  • In a relationship with Caitlyn
  • Partners with Jayce to fight against Silco's Shimmer operations
  • Motivated by the rights of the people
Viktor
  • Initially Heimerdinger's assistant at the Academy
  • Eventually partnered with Jayce in his inventions
  • Suffered from terminal illness and body modifications
  • Obsessed with the Hex Core
  • Saved by Jayce who destroyed the Hex Core
  • Supported Jayce's Piltover/Zaun peace treaty proposal
